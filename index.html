Artists' Reception: Rachel Wichlacz

From the red fall leaves of a maple tree to the simplest green blade of grass and the most vivid purple iris, the world of plants offers chromatic brilliance in the full range of visible light. Using her macro lens to capture that light, Rachel Wichlacz exhibits a rainbow of photographic floral prints.
